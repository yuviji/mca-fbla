Bug fixes, Samosa Chaat Fundraiser, and more!

  • Aditya Kirubakaran
  • October 17th, 2022

Hi everyone! We hope everyone is having a great first couple weeks to the school year, and enjoyed the first meeting we hosted! We're so excited to announce that our chapter grew to 99 members this year, a 100% increase from last year!

This honestly came as such a surprise to our whole council, and this unbelievable growth is all thanks to you! Congratulations on helping us make history this year.

We've finally gotten a chance to make some major reviews and uphauls to our site! Below are some of the newest changes / bugs we've fixed on the website.

  • If you haven't noticed yet, all main pages now have full footers at the bottom! Use the links to help you navigate to some of the most important FBLA details you'll need this year!
  • All buttons now redirect to other tabs instead of loading onto the same page.
  • A bunch of information on the site was changed, including the placement of information, refinement of already existing info, and much more!
  • All council members now have their emails linked to each of their profiles, replacing the already existing Facebook pages.
  • A bug causing the Home Page & About page to be underlined on the Membership & Community pages has now been fixed.
  • The images in the Fun w/ FBLA section are slowly being revised to accomodate the new pictures from the '22 - '23 membership year!
  • A calendar (through google calendar) will be added to the membership page shortly!
If you identify any bugs over the course of your time exploring this website, please email Aditya Kirubakaran listing what exactly the issue is!

Also! Another exciting announcement: Samosa Chaat!

If you haven't already heard, MCA FBLA is hosting a samosa chaat fundraiser on October 19th, 2022 during all lunches! We look forward to seeing you all there to enjoy a delicious bowl of Samosa Chaat for just 5 dollars! Come support your FBLA chapter!



And last but not least, please remember that SFLC is in exactly TWO weeks! Everyone that registered through the form on google classroom has been paid for. We look forward to seeing you soon! More information about SFLC will be posted soon.

Have a great day!
